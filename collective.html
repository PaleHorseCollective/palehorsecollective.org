The Initiation Path // How You Move Through The Collective

The Journey

01 // Discovery
You Find It

A TikTok decode. An Instagram symbol. A question you can't un-ask. The archive is there when you arrive.

02 // The Archive
You Read

The research library pulls you in. An hour passes. The pattern becomes visible. You find the source citations.

03 // First File
You Acquire

First file acquired. Outer Court access unlocked. You enter the community. The garment is the document.

04 // Contribution
You Build

You vote on the next drop. You submit research. You become a co-creator. The archive carries your work.

Deep contributors are invited into the Initiated tier. Revenue share begins. Ownership is real.

You Tell Others

Not because you were asked to. Because you feel you discovered something real. That is the only marketing that works.
